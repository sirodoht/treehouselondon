treehouselondon.org

Housing Co-operatives
& Intentional Communities
Directory for London, UK

This website is a list of houses that are either housing co-ops or intentional communities.

Housing co-ops are houses owned by the community and controlled by their residents.

Intentional communities are residential communities with a high degree of social cohesion and teamwork (in comparison to shared housing).

FAQ

– What qualifies as inside London?
– Any building inside a London Borough.

– How to submit an addition or a change?
– Please email admin@treehouselondon.org

– What is the license use of this?
– All information is public sourced from the websites linked. The source code of this website is open (see here).

– Why does this website exists?
– More and more people are interested in living without landlords and/or with their friends. This is a space where we can collect certain info on how to do this in London, UK.
